Getting Started

Getting started with Hystreet is really simple, and we’re with you every step of the way.

Step 1: See a Demo

First off, all you need to do is take a look at our online demo videos to see our product in action for yourself. Register on our website to view a full user journey from booking a valuation all the way through to completion.

You don’t even have to leave the comfort of your office! These videos will show you how the product works for other agents out in the real world, and outline how it can bring your business to the next level.

Step 2: Sign up

Happy with what you see? Fantastic! Get in touch with us and let's get you on board. We'll get you to fill out our on boarding questionnaire, which gives us all the information we need to start putting together your bespoke Hystreet platform.

Step 3: Let us do the hard work…

Once we have everything we need, it’s time to get your platform set up. We will liaise with your web developers and other providers to make your Hystreet platform the perfect fit for your business, installing your brand, colours, and setting up your first users, so it’s all ready for you. Sit back, relax, and know that everything is being taken care of, whilst we inform you of our progress every step of the way. You don’t start paying until everything is up and running.

Step 4: Start winning online instructions

Once everything is running and integrated with your website, you can start winning instructions and making money with online customers that very same day. It’s time to fight back against online agencies with your genuine local expertise, and a cutting-edge online platform that’s accessible to your users 24/7.
